## What to Test

### 1. Language Switching
- Go to **Settings** (gear icon in navigation)
- Change the language (9 available: English, Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, Arabic, Italian, Dutch, Japanese)
- Navigate through the app and verify text changes appropriately
- **Report:** Any text that stays in English or looks broken

### 2. Teams & Groups
- Browse the 48 qualifying teams
- Tap on a team to see details
- Check that team information displays correctly
- **Report:** Missing info, broken images, or display issues

### 3. Host Cities
- Browse the 16 host cities
- Tap on a city to view the "Command Center"
- Check sections: Stadium info, Transit, Safety, Religious Services
- **Report:** Missing content, broken links, or confusing navigation

### 4. Match Schedule
- View the full tournament schedule (104 matches)
- Filter by date or round (Group Stage, Round of 16, etc.)
- Check that times and matchups display correctly
- **Report:** Incorrect info or display problems

### 5. Knockout Bracket
- View the tournament bracket visualization
- Verify it's easy to understand the progression
- **Report:** Confusing layout or display issues

### 6. Global Watch Hubs
- Browse watch party locations by country
- Use the search to find a specific country
- Try submitting a test venue (click "Suggest a Watch Party Venue")
- **Report:** Search not working, missing countries, form issues

### 7. News Feed
- Check the tournament news section
- Verify headlines are translated in your selected language
- **Report:** News not loading or translation issues

### 8. Navigation & Usability
- Is the bottom navigation easy to use on mobile?
- Are buttons and links easy to tap with your thumb?
- Does the app feel smooth and responsive?
- **Report:** Anything confusing, slow, or hard to use

## How to Report Issues

When you find something wrong, please note:

1. **What you were trying to do**
2. **What happened instead**
3. **What device/browser you used** (e.g., iPhone 14, Safari)
4. **What language you had selected**
5. **Screenshot if possible**
